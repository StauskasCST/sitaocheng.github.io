Fields of Interest

 My research mainly focuses on how to use language models (LMs) to reasoning over structured environments (e.g. knowledge base, tables, DBs). My interests include multi-hop reasoning (e.g. question answering), LLM-agents and knowledge graph. Currently, I am focusing on the following research topics:
  • LLMs interaction with structured environments. (e.g. KB reasoning by interaction.)
  • Retrieval Augmented Generation. (e.g. retrieval of structured information by LLMs and LLM reasoning augmentation.)
  • Semantic parsing. (Translate a natural language utterance to executable queries for precise reasoning on environments.)
  • Grounded language models. (Ground a plan to structured environments.)
